ACFO Fivetran App

Amazon Selling Partner Data Connector by Anderson CFO LLC

About

ACFO Fivetran App connects your Amazon Selling Partner account to a cloud data warehouse through Fivetran. It syncs your Amazon sales, inventory, pricing, and catalog data so you can analyze your business performance with powerful reporting and analytics tools.

Built by Anderson CFO LLC, a data and analytics consultancy specializing in ecommerce operations. We help Amazon sellers turn their data into actionable insights.

Features & Services

Order Data Sync

Automatically sync order history, returns, and fulfillment data from Amazon Seller Central to your data warehouse for complete sales visibility.

Inventory Tracking

Monitor inventory levels, restock recommendations, and FBA storage metrics with up-to-date data pulled directly from the Selling Partner API.

Pricing & Catalog Data

Track competitive pricing, catalog listings, and product details to optimize your Amazon storefront and pricing strategy.

Analytics & Reporting

Once your data is in the warehouse, build custom dashboards and reports to track KPIs like revenue trends, sell-through rates, profit margins, and advertising ROI. Combine Amazon data with other sales channels for a unified view of your business.

How It Works

  1. Authorize — Connect your Amazon Seller account through a secure OAuth flow.
  2. Sync — Fivetran automatically extracts and loads your Selling Partner data into BigQuery or another supported warehouse.
  3. Analyze — Use your preferred BI tool (Looker Studio, Tableau, etc.) to build dashboards, track trends, and generate reports.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 10, 2026

Data Collection

This application accesses Amazon Selling Partner data on behalf of authorized users. Data collected includes pricing information, inventory levels, order data, and catalog information as provided by the Amazon Selling Partner API.

Use of Data

All data collected is used for business analytics and reporting purposes. Data is transmitted to a cloud data warehouse via Fivetran for analysis and reporting.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share collected data with third parties. Data is processed through Fivetran as a data pipeline service and stored in a private data warehouse. No data is shared beyond what is necessary for the operation of this service.

Data Retention

Data is retained in the data warehouse for as long as needed for business analytics purposes. Data may be deleted upon request or when no longer required.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect data during transmission and storage, including encryption and access controls.
