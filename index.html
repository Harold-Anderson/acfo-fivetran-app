ACFO Fivetran App

Anderson CFO LLC

About

ACFO Fivetran App is an internal data connector for syncing Amazon Selling Partner data to a data warehouse via Fivetran for business analytics and reporting. This app is for internal use only and is not available for purchase.

Features

  • Syncs pricing, inventory, and order data from Amazon Selling Partner API
  • Enables real-time business analytics and reporting

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 10, 2026

Data Collection

This application accesses Amazon Selling Partner data on behalf of Anderson CFO LLC. Data collected includes pricing information, inventory levels, and order data as provided by the Amazon Selling Partner API.

Use of Data

All data collected is used solely for internal business analytics and reporting purposes by Anderson CFO LLC. Data is transmitted to a data warehouse via Fivetran for analysis.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share collected data with third parties. Data is processed through Fivetran as a data pipeline service and stored in our private data warehouse. No data is shared beyond what is necessary for the operation of this internal tool.

Data Retention

Data is retained in our data warehouse for as long as it is needed for business analytics purposes. Data may be deleted upon request or when it is no longer required for business operations.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect data during transmission and storage, including encryption and access controls.
